Thank you for Playing Crank! This brief manual will help you understand what the hell kind of website you just went to!

Generate Volt units by turning the crank, one full rotation is a full crank. Upgrade the machine by purchasing vacuum tubes to help streamline your energy generation. Upgrade your vacuum tubes to transistors for more power. Unlock more technology as you crank. Buy copper coils to increase your cranking power! this description needs ALOT OF WORK

Energy modifiers such as vacuum tubes help regulate the crank and by alternating the current, helps produce a steady stream of Volt units

Mechanical upgrades such as the copper coils improve the crank's efficiency
